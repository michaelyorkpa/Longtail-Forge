Active lists are editable working lists. Completed lists are finished but can be reopened when more work is needed. Finalized lists are read-only historical records for reproducible work such as a bill of materials. Archived lists stay available for historical lookup, and deleted lists are soft-deleted.

Finalized, archived, and deleted lists do not become casual edit surfaces. Restore, reopen, duplicate, or create an active working copy when the old record needs to support new work.
