Business workspaces label the module as Procurement Lists. Personal and family workspaces label it as Shopping Lists. The underlying module ID, permissions, APIs, events, and database tables still use stable Lists terminology.

In business workspaces, a list can belong to a client or project. Selecting a project derives the client from that project, and mismatched client/project combinations are rejected. Personal and family workspaces keep client context out of the list workflow.
