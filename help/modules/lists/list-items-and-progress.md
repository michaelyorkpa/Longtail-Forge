List items are concrete things or steps to act on. Each item can carry quantity, unit, needed-by date, assignee, vendor, notes, estimated cost, actual cost, tracking ID, and purchase or order status.

Checking an item, completing an item, and marking an item as received are separate actions. This keeps execution progress clear without silently changing unrelated state. List rows and detail views show progress, open items, next needed dates, and useful next actions so work can be resumed without rebuilding context from memory.
