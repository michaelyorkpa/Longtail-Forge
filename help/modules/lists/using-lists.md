Lists are operational execution aids. Use them for grocery runs, household shopping, trip packing, project supplies, procurement, parts, bills of materials, and repeatable checklists.

A list has a title, type, status, optional business client/project context, items, item progress, source context, linked records, tags, files, and safe recovery context. Lists are not notes, tasks, bookmarks, inventory, purchasing, accounting, vendor management, manufacturing, or ERP records.
