Notifications appear from the bell icon in the navigation row. The panel shows unread activity and links to the full Notifications page.

Notifications are scoped to the current user and workspace. Read, dismiss, and preference actions affect your notification view without changing other users' notifications.

Some record screens provide a record-level notification bell. In the task dialog, clicking the heading bell follows or unfollows notifications for that task directly. A red task bell means notifications are enabled for that task.

Notification preferences are managed through shared settings surfaces. Some notifications are created by module events, and links are hidden when you cannot access the target record.
